Classic Victoria Sandwich Cake Recipe

Why is it called Victoria?

The classic Victoria Sandwich cake was named after Queen Victoria. She was a fervant fan of afternoon teas and regularly ate a slice of cake with her tea. Buckingham Palace Pastry Chef's always make Victoria sandwich cakes at Royal Garden Parties. You may have also seen the cake in a few period dramas including Downton Abbey or Bridgerton! It is delicious and the recipe is very accessible to bakers, amateurs or pros!
